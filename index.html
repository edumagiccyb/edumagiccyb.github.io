EduMagic FAQs

What is EduMagic?

EduMagic is a company specialising in creating innovative software solutions exclusively for educational institutions to streamline operations and improve efficiency.

What services does EduMagic offer?

EduMagic develops tailored apps for educational institutions, including tools for data management, photo documentation, and campus organisation, such as CampusTag.

Who can use EduMagic's products?

Schools, colleges, universities, and any educational institutions looking for efficient software solutions can use EduMagic’s products.

What makes EduMagic different from other software providers?

EduMagic focuses exclusively on educational institutions, creating specialized solutions designed to address their unique challenges and requirements.

Does EduMagic provide support and training?

Yes, EduMagic offers support and guidance to ensure institutions can make the most of their software.

What is CampusTag?

CampusTag is EduMagic’s geo-tagged photo management app that allows institutions to capture, store, and search photos with metadata like location, block name, and time.
